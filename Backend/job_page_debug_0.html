General Manager, Behavioural & Workplace Health

Send this job :
    • Link copied to clipboard!

    Organisation : Caraniche

    Location : Melbourne (Melbourne CBD)

    Work type : Full-time

    Profession : Management

    Sector : Mental Health

    Salary type : Annual Package

    Salary : 140000-185000 per Year

    Apply now

    About the organisation

    Organisation Name : Caraniche

    About the role

    • Senior Executive Leadership role; Commercial Focus
    • Leading Behavioural / Workplace services organisation
    • Hybrid; $140k - $165k + Super + Salary Packaging

    The Company

    Caraniche is a specialist provider of psychological and behavioural health services, committed to creating better outcomes for individuals, workplaces, and communities. With decades of experience across the justice, health, and workplace sectors, they deliver evidence-based solutions that make a lasting impact.

    The Opportunity

    We are seeking an accomplished and visionary General Manager, Behavioural and Workplace Health (BWH), to lead the delivery and strategic growth of the workplace and behavioural health services. This leadership position will shape the future of the BWH portfolio and ensure the high quality behavioural mental health clinical services which are preventative and responsive to mitigate against workplace psychosocial hazards and strengthen workforce wellbeing to a broad range of high risk workforces in health, justice, courts and government sectors.

    In this diverse and exciting new position, you will be responsible for ensuring excellence in ongoing service delivery, clinical governance, risk management, and financial performance. Manage multidisciplinary teams and support line managers, oversee contract compliance, reporting, and continuous improvement across all service agreements and involvement in tender preparation and delivery. Build strong relationships with key stakeholders, drive business development, and support Caraniche’s reputation in the workplace health sector.

    What You Bring

    • Tertiary qualifications in a health discipline (e.g. organisational psychology, allied health, medical)
    • Qualifications in business, leadership, or commercial management
    • Senior leadership experience in a health or human services environment (General Management/Senior Operations Management level)
    • Strong business and commercial acumen to lead sustainable, commercial viability and grow and scale across a national footprint.
    • Proven ability to lead and manage diverse teams
    • Strong business development and strategic insight
    • Outstanding stakeholder engagement and communication skills
    • A collaborative leadership style aligned to Caraniche’s values and purpose

    Why Join?

    At Caraniche, they value innovation, integrity, excellence and partnership. This is a fabulous opportunity to join this wonderful organisation that is in a high-growth phase. By joining Caraniche, you will enjoy:
    • A collaborative, forward-thinking senior leadership team and a dynamic, flexible and dedicated manager
    • The opportunity to influence the strategic direction and future growth of a leading workplace health provider working within a flexible, caring, and friendly environment
    • Flexible work arrangements/hybrid role (2-3 days in the office and 2 days from home)
    • Fully reimbursed travel to other sites
    • A competitive salary will be offered for the role, depending on experience of between $140k – $165k + Super + Salary Packaging and other employee benefits.. 
    To apply, please click the “Apply” tab below to submit your resume in Word format. To confidentially discuss this role further, please feel free to call Lisa Webb from Johnson Recruitment on 0428 679 060.
    This is a retained, exclusive assignment, and any direct applications or referrals will be forwarded to Johnson Recruitment.
